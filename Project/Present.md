## Overview

Your project will need to be planned, tested, and presented to the class to demonstrate your understanding of structured programming. Be sure to focus on using `selection` and `iteration` structures to make your project work.

## Project Criteria

Your task is to:

* Share the project you built with CircuitPlayground Express and Python.
* Demonstrate how users can interact with the project.
* Explain your program:
    * Explain the IPO modules your project includes:
        * Explain the inputs you chose to use.
        * Explain how data from the inputs are processed.
        * Explain how the CircuitPlayground Express outputs to the user.
    * Explain how you used selection control structures.
    * Explain how you used iteration control structures.

Record a video no longer than 1 minute in length demonstrating what your project does. The video does not need to be a studio level production, but it should clearly show what inputs are used to control your project, and the outputs produced (e.g. Neopixels, speaker).
